Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate DPI?

To calculate DPI, divide the number of pixels moved by the number of inches moved. This gives you the DPI of your mouse or screen.

What is DPI?

DPI stands for Dots Per Inch and refers to the sensitivity of your mouse or display device. A higher DPI indicates greater sensitivity.

Why is DPI important for gaming?

DPI is crucial in gaming as it determines how fast your cursor or pointer moves. A higher DPI means quicker movement, while a lower DPI offers more precision.

What is a Click Speed Calculator?

A Click Speed Calculator is a tool that measures how many times you can click your mouse within a specific time limit, usually in seconds.

How does the Click Speed Test work?

The Click Speed Test starts when you click a designated area on the screen, counting the number of clicks within a set time (e.g., 5 seconds, 10 seconds).

What is a good Click Speed?

A good click speed varies, but an average clicker scores between 5-10 clicks per second (CPS). Faster clickers can go beyond 12 CPS.
