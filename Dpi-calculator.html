Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate DPI?

To calculate DPI, divide the number of pixels moved by the number of inches moved. This gives you the DPI of your mouse or screen.

What is DPI?

DPI stands for Dots Per Inch and refers to the sensitivity of your mouse or display device. A higher DPI indicates greater sensitivity.

Why is DPI important for gaming?

DPI is crucial in gaming as it determines how fast your cursor or pointer moves. A higher DPI means quicker movement, while a lower DPI offers more precision.
