What is eDPI?

The eDPI (Effective Dots Per Inch) Calculator is a tool used by gamers to determine the true sensitivity of their mouse. It is calculated using the formula:

eDPI = DPI × In-game Sensitivity

This allows players to have a standardized measurement of their mouse settings, making it easier to compare sensitivity across different DPI values.

Why is eDPI Important for Gaming?

  • Consistency Across Games: Ensures the same sensitivity across different FPS games like Valorant and CS:GO.
  • Competitive Advantage: Professional gamers use optimized eDPI values for better accuracy and control.
  • Better Aim and Tracking: Helps in achieving precise aim movement and smooth tracking of targets.
  • Avoiding Overcorrection: Prevents mouse movements from being too slow or too fast, enhancing reaction times.

By using an eDPI calculator, gamers can fine-tune their settings for optimal performance and achieve the perfect balance between speed and accuracy.


Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is eDPI?

eDPI (Effective Dots Per Inch) is calculated by multiplying your mouse DPI by your in-game sensitivity. It helps compare sensitivity settings across different DPI values.

How do I calculate eDPI?

You can calculate eDPI using the formula: eDPI = DPI × In-game Sensitivity. Use our calculator above for quick results.

What is a good eDPI for gaming?

It depends on the game. For FPS games like CS:GO and Valorant, most professional players use an eDPI between 200 and 800.
